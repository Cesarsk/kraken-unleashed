Privacy policy

Mostly local by design.

Kraken Unleashed does not require an account, does not ship ads, and does not include analytics or usage telemetry. Most activity happens locally on your machine.

Quick summary
  • No user account required
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • Optional GIF search can contact a third-party service

Data processed locally

  • GIF files you import or download into the app gallery
  • Saved placement presets for GIFs
  • Application state and local settings such as startup preferences

This data stays on your computer so the app can function. It is not automatically transmitted to the maintainer.

Third-party network requests

If the in-app GIF search feature is available in your build, search queries and related requests are sent to the Klipy GIF service so the app can show results and download the GIF you selected.

  • Your search query
  • Normal technical request metadata from your system and network
  • The request needed to download the selected GIF

External links

If you click a link in the app or on this website, the destination opens in your default browser and is governed by that site's own privacy practices.

Device communication

Kraken Unleashed communicates locally with supported Kraken hardware over USB to detect devices, adjust settings, recover the display state, and transfer GIF content. That USB communication does not by itself send your data to the maintainer.

Release scanning

Release artifacts may be uploaded by the maintainer to VirusTotal during the release process for malware scanning. This applies to packaged release files created by the maintainer, not to end-user runtime behavior inside the app.

Data sharing

Except for the third-party requests described above, Kraken Unleashed does not automatically send personal data or app usage data to the maintainer or to other third parties.

Your choices

  • Avoid third-party GIF search by not using the in-app search feature
  • Use local GIF files instead of downloading them through the app
  • Review the public source code to verify application behavior

Data retention and updates

Local files and settings remain on your device until you remove them or uninstall the application. This policy may be updated when project behavior changes, with the latest version published in the repository.
